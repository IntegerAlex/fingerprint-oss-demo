Privacy Policy & Terms

We believe in complete transparency about how we identify devices

Our Privacy Commitment

At Fingerprint OSS, we're committed to providing a transparent and privacy-focused approach to device identification. Our demo showcases technology that can help protect websites from fraud while respecting user privacy.

Important: This is a demonstration website. The data displayed is shown to you only and is not stored, shared, or sold to any third parties.

What Information We Collect

The demo shows you what technical information can be collected from your browser and device. This may include:

Browser type & version
Operating system
Screen resolution
Time zone
Language settings
Hardware capabilities

None of this information personally identifies you. It's a digital "fingerprint" that helps distinguish between different devices.

How This Information Is Used

In real-world applications, this technology can be used to:

  • Detect and prevent fraud and account takeovers
  • Protect users from bots and automated attacks
  • Improve security without requiring personal information
  • Create a safer online experience
Your control: Most browsers offer settings to limit tracking. While this may affect the accuracy of fingerprinting, we respect your choice to use these settings.
